lasagna

Description:

La lasaña es un plato italiano clásico que consiste en capas de pasta, salsa de carne (ragú), salsa bechamel y queso, generalmente mozzarella o parmesano.

Ingredientes:

  • 1 pound lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound Italian sausage, removed from casings
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 (28 ounce) cans crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 tablespoons flour
  • 4 cups milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, brown ground beef and Italian sausage over medium heat. Drain off any excess grease.
  4. Add onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, pepper, and parsley. Bring to a simmer and cook for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. In a medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan cheese. Mix well.
  7. To make the bechamel sauce, melt the butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Whisk in the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes, until a paste forms.
  8. Gradually whisk in the milk, a little at a time, until smooth. Bring to a simmer, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  10. Layer with lasagna noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers, ending with a layer of noodles, bechamel sauce, and mozzarella.
  11. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
  12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  13. Let stand for 10-15 minutes before cutting and serving.
